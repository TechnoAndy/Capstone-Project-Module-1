TWO IS BETTER THAN ONE
MARRIAGE SEMINAR
2022

Marriage is full of many ups and downs, adventures, and incredible memories with the person you love. Join us for an amazing experience.

2022.11.11(FRI) ~ 12(SAT)

@ Oaklands Inn and Conference Centre, Randburg

Main Program

  • Ice Breaker

    • Fun activites for everyone to get to know eachother and observe how couples work together

  • Group Activity

    • Couples will be grouped into 4 pairs to share experiences on specific topics

  • Supper

    • A 4 course nutritous supper will be served

  • Topic

    • An outlined topic will be discussed

  • Q & A

    • The speakers will try their best to answer any questions.
